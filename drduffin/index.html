Hello friends of Dr. Duffin! Dr. Duffin is giving her last med school lecture (ever) to the class of Meds 2020, on April 18th. We are honouring her contributions to QMED by assembling a video collage by her students and presenting her with a small booklet of messages.

Instructions (Computer)

  1. Click "Record Video" above
  2. If "Adobe Flash Player Settings" pops up, click "Allow" and then "Close"
  3. If your browser asks, allow it to use your webcam
  4. To start recording, click the RED CIRCLE in the bottom-left.
  5. Say your message to Dr. Duffin.
  6. Click the same button again to stop the recording. It will upload to us automatically.
  7. Thank you!

Instructions (Smartphone)

  1. Click "1. Record a video" (bottom of page)
  2. Record the video with your phone's camera, as you normally would
  3. Review the video. If you're happy with it, click the checkmark. If you want to re-do, click X.
  4. Click on "2. Upload" (bottom-most button) to send the video to us.
  5. Thank you!
